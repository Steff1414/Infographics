The Ground Rules

01.

The Product

It is not just a model name. It is the specific item: Style + Colour + Size.

02.

Name vs ID

The Article/SKU is just a tracking number. It is the passport number, not the person.

03.

Dimensions

Colour and Size are ingredients of the product, not separate products themselves.

04.

Grouping

Everything is sorted via Classifications. Like organizing the finest leather hides.

Data Structure

Building Blocks

Product

The Sellable Item

"The pair of gloves the customer holds. Defined by its unique look (colour) and fit (size)."

Defining Attributes
Colour Size Brand Collection
EX: Heli Ski - Grey - Size 9

Article (SKU)

The Logistic ID

"The technical ID for the warehouse. It points to the Product, but it isn't the Product itself."

Defining Attributes
Article No SKU
EX: 123456-GR-09

Collection

The Family

"A long-term home for products. Tells us the product's purpose, whether 'Alpine Pro' or 'Dress'."

Defining Attributes
Name Purpose
EX: Alpine Pro, Sport

Season

The Time

"When is this relevant? Seasons help us plan launches and manage relevance over time."

Defining Attributes
Code Year
EX: AW 2026
