The Ground Rules

1. The Product

A "Product" isn't just a style name. It's the specific thing you buy: Style + Colour + Size.

2. The Name vs ID

The Article/SKU is just a tracking number. It's not the product's soul; it's just its passport number.

3. Ingredients

Colour and Size are dimensions (ingredients) of the product, not separate products themselves.

4. Grouping

Everything is organized using one simple system: Classifications. Like sorting socks, but smarter.

The Essentials

Our Building Blocks

Product

The Sellable Item

"This is the pair of gloves the customer holds in their hands. It is defined by its specific look (colour) and fit (size)."

Inside this box (Attributes):
Colour Size Brand Collection
Eg: Heli Ski - Grey - Size 9

Article (SKU)

The Logistic ID

"The technical ID for the warehouse. Think of it as the barcode sticker. It points to the Product, but it isn't the Product."

Inside this box (Attributes):
Article Number SKU
Eg: 123456-GR-09

Collection

The Family

"A long-term home for products. Whether it's for 'Alpine Pro' or 'Dress', this tells us the product's purpose and style."

Inside this box (Attributes):
Name Purpose
Eg: Alpine Pro, Sport

Season

The Time

"When is this product relevant? Seasons come and go, helping us plan launches and manage relevance over time."

Inside this box (Attributes):
Code Year
Eg: Autumn/Winter 2026
